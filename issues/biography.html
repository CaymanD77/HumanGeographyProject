Federico Caprotti

Federico Caprotti is a Professor of Human Geography at the University of Exeter in the United Kingdom. His research focuses on urban sustainability, smart cities, and the intersection of technology and urban life. Caprotti has explored how smart city projects are reshaping urban governance and social inclusion. His academic work critiques the idea that smart cities improve urban life overall. He highlights the risks of fragmented policies and overlooked social inequalities. Caprotti has published in geography journals, including Progress in Human Geography and Environment and Planning D: Society and Space. Caprotti's work emphasizes the need for cities to balance their technological advancements with more inclusive governance and strategies that prioritize public engagement and equality.
